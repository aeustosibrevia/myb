# MYB — Make Yourself Better

## Overview
**MYB** is a web application designed to help people improve themselves by focusing on **health**, **learning**, and **breaking bad habits**.
The platform combines **motivation**, **AI-powered personalization**, **progress tracking**, and **gamification** to support long-term growth and lifestyle changes.

The core idea is not just to fight bad habits, but to **replace them with healthier and more productive routines**, while keeping users motivated and engaged.

---

## Goals
- Provide a **personalized assistant** for health, learning, and habit management.
- Use **AI models** to generate customized workout plans, diets, and study resources.
- Make personal growth **engaging** with gamification, achievements, and rankings.
- Help users clearly **visualize their progress** with statistics and motivational feedback.

---

### Health
- Personalized **training programs** and **meal plans** based on user goals.
- **Progress tracker**: manual input for workouts, meals, and sleep.
- AI-generated **tips for exercise and nutrition**.

### Learning
- **Default learning goals** with the option to add custom ones.
- **Breaking down goals into micro-tasks** with deadlines.
- Daily challenges and **reminder system**.
- AI-based **recommendation of educational resources**.
- Learning progress dashboard.

### Bad Habits
- **Habit-free tracker**: count days without a habit.
- **Savings calculator**: show how much money and time was saved (e.g., “In 30 days without smoking you saved 1200 UAH — that’s a ticket to Budapest”).
- **Motivational notifications** and streak-based encouragement.

---

## Global Features
- **Gamification**:
  - Achievements for milestones: *“21 days without smoking”, “30 days of training”*.
  - Ranking system with **visualized tiers** (Wood -> Bronze -> Silver -> Gold -> Diamond).
- **User system**:
  - Registration & login via email, Google, or social networks.
  - Personal dashboard with progress graphs, streaks, achievements.
- **Motivational push notifications** sent to email.
- (Optional) **Telegram bot** for mobile-friendly usage.

---

## AI Integration
MYB will leverage AI to provide a **personalized experience**:
- **Health**: generating workout and nutrition plans.
- **Learning**: recommending tutorials, books, and online courses tailored to goals.
- **Habits**: generating motivational messages and replacement strategies.
- **Analytics**: summarizing user progress with natural language insights.
